Privacy Policy — NiBoard (iOS & macOS)

Last updated: 11 Feb 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use NiBoard on iOS and macOS.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • NiBoard for iOS
  • NiBoard for macOS

User-Generated Content

All drawings, notes, and other content you create in NiBoard are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, access, or store your user-generated content on external servers or cloud services.

Personal Information

We do not collect information that directly identifies you, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address

NiBoard for iOS

Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

NiBoard for iOS uses TelemetryDeck to collect privacy-friendly, pseudonymous analytics to help us understand app performance and improve features.

The analytics data may include:

  • App version
  • iOS version
  • Device type
  • Country/region (approximate)
  • Basic usage events (for example: tool selections, mode/layout changes, canvas clear actions, saves/shares, and session duration categories)

Analytics data is pseudonymous and is used only to understand feature usage and improve the app. We do not use analytics for advertising, cross-app tracking, or building user profiles.

We do not collect:

  • Your drawings or other user-generated content
  • Names, email addresses, or other direct identifiers

We do not use analytics data to identify you.

For more information, please review TelemetryDeck’s Privacy Policy: TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

NiBoard uses RevenueCat to manage and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions.

RevenueCat may process information such as:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Product identifiers
  • Anonymous device or app instance identifiers required for subscription validation

We do not send personal identifiers (such as your name or email) to RevenueCat.

For more information, see RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

NiBoard for macOS

NiBoard for macOS stores all user-generated content locally on your device.

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

NiBoard for macOS uses RevenueCat to manage and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions.

RevenueCat may process information such as:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Product identifiers
  • Anonymous device or app instance identifiers required for subscription validation

We do not send personal identifiers (such as your name or email) to RevenueCat.

For more information, see RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Analytics

NiBoard for macOS does not use TelemetryDeck analytics, unless stated otherwise on this page. If we enable analytics for the macOS app in the future, this policy will be updated.

Apple Services

We may receive limited, aggregated information from Apple’s developer services (such as App Store analytics), which can include:

  • Install date
  • Territory (country/region)
  • Device type
  • Purchase events

This information is used only to understand app performance and improve the experience.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, along with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@enidapps.com
